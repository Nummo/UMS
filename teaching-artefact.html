An idea of an 'Open World Map' could use the concept of visa-free travel:

  • Consider the passport(s) you hold
  • Passport(s) members of your family have
  • Passport(s) your friends have
Passport Index is a website which provides details about visa based on passport held/using: https://www.passportindex.org/comparebyPassport.php
